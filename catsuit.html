The Groove is a hand-cut slitweave, a lattice of slits woven back together down the front and sides, from rib to ankle. It is cut on a four-way-stretch base for a locked-in, high-rise fit that moves with spins, drops and floor work, then holds its shape all night. Finished with your choice of cloth or metal ties.

  • Cotton-blend stretch base, four-way stretch, opaque core panels
  • Every slit cut and woven by hand, no two identical
  • Cloth or metal ties; tie-dye and bleach-dye finishes available
  • High-rise, full-length. Model is 5 foot 9 wearing a size S

Hand wash cold, inside out. Lay flat to dry. Do not bleach, iron the cutouts, or tumble dry. Skip fabric softener to protect the stretch and the weave.
