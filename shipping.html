SHIPPING & RETURNS

Getting it to you

Shipping

Free standard shipping on orders over $75. Under that, a flat rate is calculated at checkout, with express options if you need it sooner.

Ready-to-ship pieces leave the studio within a few days of your order. Made-to-order builds are cut and laced just for you, so they take about two to four weeks before they ship.

Returns

All sales are final. Every slitweave is cut and woven by hand, so each piece is one of a kind and cannot be restocked. Please check the size guide before you order, and if you are between sizes or want an exact fit, build it made to order with your own measurements.

If a piece arrives damaged or there is a mistake on our end, that is on us. Reach out through the studio within a week of delivery and we will make it right.

Care

Hand wash cold, inside out, and lay flat to dry. No bleach, no tumble dry, no ironing the cutouts, and skip fabric softener to protect the stretch and the weave. The full routine lives in the care guide.
