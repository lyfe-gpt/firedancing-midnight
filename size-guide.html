How to measure

Bust. Around the fullest part of your chest, tape level and snug.

Waist. Around the narrowest part of your midsection, usually just above the navel.

Hip. Around the fullest part of your seat, feet together.

Between two sizes? The stretch base is forgiving, so most people size down for a locked-in second-skin fit or up for more coverage. If you want it exact, skip the guesswork and build it made to order with your own measurements.

Every piece is hand-cut and one of a kind, so all sales are final. Check your numbers against the chart before you order, and reach out through the studio if you are unsure.
